Who We Are

We are two freelance developers trained in a diverse range of Computer Science fields with experience developing software in a vast range of industries including the Aviation, Advertising, Banking and Forensic industries; as well as R&D work in machine learning and computer vision.

What We Offer

We can help you realise your software, app and web-based needs and can cover the full range of development stages from initial feasibility studies, design and prototyping, to fully-fledged customer-facing end products and post-launch maintenance.
